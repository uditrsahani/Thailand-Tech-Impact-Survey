Impact Of Digital Technology On Traditional Markets And Local Businesses Survey Form.


Welcome to the GitHub repository for the "Impact Of Digital Technology On Traditional Markets And Local Businesses Survey Form" project. This repository contains the source code and assets for a web-based survey form designed to gather insights about the impact of digital technology on traditional markets and local businesses in Thailand.

About the Project:
In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, traditional markets and local businesses face various challenges due to the advent of digital technology. This project aims to conduct a comprehensive survey to better understand these challenges and opportunities. The survey collects valuable information from participants, including their personal details and thoughtful responses to survey questions.

The survey covers topics such as:
- Personal information: Name, Age, Gender, and Province
- Multiple choice questions related to the impact of digital technology
- Open-ended question to gather qualitative insights

Technical Details:
The survey form is built using a combination of web technologies to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience.

1. HTML, CSS, JavaScript: These technologies are used to create the user interface and ensure responsive design. HTML provides the structure, CSS handles the styling, and JavaScript enhances interactivity and performs form validation on the frontend.

2. PHP and MySQL: To manage and store the collected survey data, PHP is used as the backend scripting language, and MySQL serves as the database system. This combination ensures secure storage of the survey responses and facilitates data analysis.

3. Responsive Design: The form is designed to be accessible and visually appealing across various devices and screen sizes, making it easy for users to participate using their preferred devices.
